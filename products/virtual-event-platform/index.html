Frequently Asked Questions

What is virtual event platform?

Virtual event platform also know as online platform for virtual events is an internet-based online platform that allows people to organize and attend events virtually via laptop, mobile as well as desktop. It also provides powerful tools and features that allow organizers to create a custom engaging virtual event platform and connect people around the world.

How do virtual event platforms work?

Virtual event platforms operate by enabling digital experiences via integrated built-in webcasting, collaboration with webinar software or even a combination of both. These platforms also provides a range of engaging features such as gamification, emoji reactions, moderated Q&A with upvoting, poll & quizzes, public & private chats, attendance tracking and broadcast pre-recorded videos as Live.

Is Micepad suitable for virtual events of all sizes?
Yes, Micepad guarantees a fast and reliable experience, whether you’re hosting 20 or 20,000 attendees, making it scalable for various event sizes.
Does Micepad provide analytics and reports for post virtual event analysis?

Yes, Micepad offers powerful analytics tools that allow you to track and analyze all event data after the event, providing valuable insights for future improvements.

Can I use Micepad for personalized virtual or hybrid event experiences in Singapore?

Absolutely! Micepad allows attendees to customize their experience by adding their favorite sessions and content for quick access, providing a personalized touch to their event journey not limited to Singapore but also worldwide.

How do I choose a virtual event platform or hybrid event platform?

The ideal virtual event platform should offer agenda management, speaker management features such as Live Q&A, Multimedia Library, and Public And Private Chat to ensure a seamless well-planned experience for organizers & attendees.

Can I use my preferred live streaming service with Micepad?

Yes, Micepad allows you to use its built-in streaming service or popular options like Zoom, Facebook Live, YouTube, Vimeo, RTMP, and even Singapore-based streaming platforms to broadcast your video globally.
