Frequently Asked Questions

What is check in app and guest manager app?

A check-in app, also referred to as a guest manager app, serves as a valuable tool for event organizers. It plays a important role in managing event check-ins and ticket scanning, providing insights into the number of attendees at both virtual and hybrid events. With this Event Check-In App, you have the capability to upload your guest list and benefit from real-time name search and check-in on an unlimited number of devices, making attendee management and tracking seamless and efficient.

How do you track people at events?

Attendees can utilize their mobile devices to scan QR codes, storing the data within an app (also known as mobile event app) or spreadsheet application. This method streamlines the event tracking process, as guests can easily present their digital tickets by scanning them with their mobile phones.

How do event organizers keep track of guest at the event?

Micepad check in apps for events provides a modern way to keep track of the attendees such as 

  1. Self-Service Check-In: Allows attendees quickly check in by scanning the QR code and grabbing their badge as fast as possible. 
  2. On-Demand Badge Printing: Pre-print badges with event branding or on-demand during onsite QR check-in
  3. Offline Capabilities: we will help you to check in guests and print badges
Can Micepad’s event check in app handle last-minute registration?

Yes, we can handle last-minute changes and we are flexible enough and make the registration process easy and quick for added new guests whether is walk-ins or last-minute registration.
