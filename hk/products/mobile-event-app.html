Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mobile Event App?

Mobile event application is software that enables event planners to customize mobile apps for events without hands-on coding. The organizers can host to digitally or manually record the attendance of attendees whenever they are. Normally the app features include schedules, record attendee & exhibitor information, and event timing among other details.

What does an Event App do?

A mobile event app creates several numerous avenues for communication and interaction with the attendees. Event planners can enhance the conference experience via interaction features provided by the app such as displaying top questions to the audience, sending messages and updating the latest info to all attendees and speakers to make sure everyone stays informed and up-to-date.

How do I choose an event app?

Selecting the right mobile event app is crucial for ensuring the success of the event. Follow these steps to make an informed decision:

Phase I – Research and Discovery in Hong Kong:

Step 1 – Detail the needs of your event:

Identify your event’s specific requirements, such as what type of attendee engagement, event promotion, data analytics, and branding. Understanding your goals is the first step in choosing the right app.

 

Step 2 – Set A Budget And Pricing Model:

Determine your budget constraints and pricing preferences. Ensure that the app you choose aligns with your financial considerations.

 

Step 3 – Research Roundup:

Conduct thorough research to find potential event app providers. Explore their features, customer reviews, and case studies to gauge their suitability for your event.

 

Step 4 – Test Drive the App:

Before committing, take advantage of free trials or demos offered by app providers. Test their user interface, customization options, and ease of use.

 

Step 5 – Branding and Customization:

Ensure the app allows for branding and customization to align with your event’s theme and identity.

 

Step 6 – Customer Service:

Evaluate the quality of customer support provided by the app provider. Responsive customer service can be invaluable during the planning and execution of your event.

 

Step 7 – Sign The Deal:

After comparing your options and considering your budget, sign an agreement with your chosen event app provider.

 

Step 8 – Building Your App:

Collaborate with your chosen provider to build your customized event app. Ensure it meets all the requirements you identified in the first step.

By following these steps, you’ll be well-prepared to choose the ideal event app for your needs. For a comprehensive event app solution tailored to the Hong Kong market, explore Micepad’’s Mobile Event App.

What are the benefits of having Mobile Event App?

The top 3 benefits includes below:

Effective communication between attendees and exhibitors. The app can be used to send push notifications, chat with other attendees, and book appointments with exhibitors. This can help to improve communication and networking opportunities at the event.

Easy way to find sponsors and partners. The app can be used to view a list of sponsors and partners, and to learn more about their products and services. This can help attendees to connect with potential business partners.

Increased engagement and participation. The app can be used to create interactive activities and contests, which can help to increase engagement and participation at the event and improved event ROI. A mobile event app can help to improve event ROI by reducing costs and increasing attendance.
