3 New Features To Power Up Your Virtual Events

micepad
September 21, 2020
Product Updates
1 min read

Create and brand your business event with Micepad.

Looking to up your Virtual Events game? Look no further. 

We have just released 3 brand new features to enhance the Virtual Events experience for organisers, attendees and exhibitors alike.

1. Simultaneous Interpretation

Real-time translation of audio into your native language with Simultaneous Interpretation. Host a multilingual conference today!

Ready to livestream your event to a multilingual audience? With the click of a button, attendees can use Simultaneous Interpretation to translate audio in real time. Languages include English, Chinese, French, Japanese, Korean, Malay and more.

P.S. We integrate with major streaming platforms such as Zoom, YouTube and Facebook Live.

2. Exhibitors and Sponsors

Smarter, effective networking for Exhibitors and Sponsors to engage potential customers.

Exhibitors and sponsors can showcase their services and products with their own customisable branded pages, with personalised content and easy access to company representatives.

From hosting virtual conferences to job fairs, trade shows and education fairs, rest assured that our friendly team is ready to guide and support you towards a successful virtual exhibition event.

3. Private Chat

Exhibitor representatives can secure leads by striking a conversation with multiple event attendees simultaneously. Opt between text chat or video call for a more personal connection.

Exhibitor representatives can strike a conversation with attendees to discuss value exchange and networking opportunities.
