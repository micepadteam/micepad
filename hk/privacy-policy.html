Privacy Policy

Effective on Jun 22, 2022

Welcome to Micepad’s Privacy Statement. Your right to privacy and online security is important. This Privacy Statement describes our collection, protection, disclosure, and use of your information. This Privacy Statement applies to to all of Micepad’s websites, sub-domain, mobile applications, and/or online services (each individually, and collectively, the “Service”). It covers your personal information that is provided or collected through the our services and how we use that information in connection with our services. The use of your personal information under this policy is an integral part of Micepad’s “Services”. You are not required by law to provide us your personal information, and therefore providing your information is entirely up to you. However, if you do not provide us the necessary information, we will not be able to deliver our service effectively. Therefore, if you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not access or otherwise use Micepad’s “Services”.

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time, to adapt it to changes in our “Services”, to how Micepad operates, and to regulatory and technological requirements. We will be provide notice about any changes in our policy within a reasonable time to allow you to review the changes and decide whether you wish to continue using our “Services”.

1. Information Collected
1.1 Website Information Collected from Customers

To monitor how you use our website and admin panel, we might make use of cookies and other tracking technologies. We may keep track of things like how often you visit our site or the pages you browse. This information helps us with both service and security audits. We will not be able to identify you specifically as some of this data may be statistical or aggregated. For additional information, see “OUR USE OF COOKIES” in the section below.

Micepad will obtain data for the Micepad App regarding your event, such as the agenda, speaker biographies, documents, general event details, and floor plans. Only pre-approved event participants are granted access to this limited information. For further details, see “OUR USE OF EVENT DATA” in the section below.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Services, express interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, when you participate in activities on the Services, or otherwise when you contact us.   • Job Titles
  • Contact Preferences
  • Contact or Authentication Data

You will be required to provide us certain information, including but not limited to your name, email address, company name, contact information, and event details, in order to successfully message us when you subscribe to our content, send us a “demo request,” or make any other type of “contact form submission” within our site and Social Media. 1.8 Your Data Protection Rights Under General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. Micepad aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data. If you wish to be informed what Personal Data we hold about you and if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

In certain circumstances, you have the following data protection rights:

  • The right to access, update or to delete the information we have on you. Whenever made possible, you can access, update or request deletion of your Personal Data directly within your account settings section. If you are unable to perform these actions yourself, please contact us to assist you.
  • The right of rectification. You have the right to have your information rectified if that information is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • The right to object. You have the right to object to our processing of your Personal Data.
  • The right of restriction. You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to data portability. You have the right to be provided with a copy of the information we have on you in a structured, machine-readable and commonly used format.
  • The right to withdraw consent. You also have the right to withdraw your consent at any time where Micepad relied on your consent to process your personal information.

Please note that we may ask you to verify your identity before responding to such requests. You have the right to complain to a Data Protection Authority about our collection and use of your Personal Data. For more information, please contact your local data protection authority in the European Economic Area (EEA).

2 Usage of Data 
2.1 Our Use of Cookies  

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. This information is used to track visitor use of the Site and to compile statistical reports on Site activity. Our site features require Cookies to be enabled to function.

We want to inform users that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the App we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the App when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

2.2 Our Use of Event Data

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to name, email address, company name, contact information, event details or user image. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

Event data collected from our customers and information provided by pre-approved App users such as users’ email, full name and user images are solely used on our web or mobile App and are treated in accordance to instructions given to us.

  • All user information and user images that are acquired from customers or pre-approved event participants for ID verification or audio-visual content for video conferencing purposes will be handled securely and access to this data are strictly limited.
  • All information is treated as classified and restricted unless specifically instructed otherwise. You will be consulted to decide on the level of access to information and to provide us with a list of approved users.
  • We will not, without your express consent, supply this information to any third party.
  • We may release your information only when we believe the release is appropriate to comply with the law or in connection with any ongoing legal proceedings as required by the law.

If a user’s image is requested for their profile information, such as for ID verification or audio-visual content for video conferencing, only registered users are permitted to upload it. The user images that are acquired are solely used for the event that is mentioned on the Micepad App. Without permission, user images will not be distributed to other websites or parties.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We are dedicated to ensuring the confidentiality of your information, and we have taken the necessary organizational, technical, and security precautions to protect it.

  • We keep all of the information you provide us on our secured servers.
  • Encryption technology will be used to safeguard any data transactions made through our website and App.
  • We won’t ask you for your password; it is your responsibility to keep the password you use to access our services private (except when you log in to our services)

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed 100% secure, so we cannot promise or guarantee other unauthorized parties will not be able to defeat our security or improperly access your information. We will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our Services at your own risk. You should only access our Services within a secure environment.

Information that we process for any purpose or purposes shall not be kept for longer than is necessary for the purpose or those purposes.

Customers using our self-service event services are free to delete their event information at their own discretion.
