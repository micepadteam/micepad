Top 5 Reasons Event Check-In Softwares are a Game Changer

micepad
July 4, 2024
Industry Insights
8 min read

Online event registration is all the rave right now, but what about event check-ins too?

Slowly but surely, they are also gathering a lot of momentum and that will continue into the next decade. If you are still wondering why that is so, here are some compelling reasons to include an online event check-in software into your next event planning!

TL;DR

What To Consider When Choosing an Event Check-In App?
When picking an event check-in app, focus on ease of use and customization to streamline registration. Pre-event preparation and efficient check-in processes are crucial, as are real-time data sync, 24/7 support, and the ability to collect feedback and analyze post-event metrics.

Benefits That Make Event Check-In Software Worth All The Hype
Event check-in softwares enhances the attendee experience with fast, intuitive check-ins, saves on paper, and provides better data management. It improves event ROI and productivity by reducing the need for extra staff and offers valuable insights for future event planning.

What Is An Event Check-in App?

An event check-in app is your go-to tool for effortlessly managing onsite attendee registration. Event check-in apps are specialized mobile apps designed to make sure your guests can enter the event smoothly and quickly. Imagine scanning tickets, checking badges, or using QR codesall from your smartphone. Its super easy to use and ensures everyone gets in without a hitch, whether youre hosting a big conference or a cozy local gathering.

Beyond just speeding up the check-in process, this app gives you real-time insights into how your event is flowing. You can track attendee movements and preferences, which helps you tweak things on the spot to make sure everything runs like clockwork.

With a check-in app, you are likely to create a hassle-free experience that leaves a great first impression for anyone attending your event. Both when organizing a major industry summit and a small community meetup, a reliable check-in app should be your secret weapon for making your event a success.

Why Your Next Event Should Use Check-In App

There are five compelling reasons why your next even should use check-in app.

1. Improve Attendee Experience

What is the first thing the attendee experiences when they get to your event? The check-in counter, of course.

Now, no matter what else you have planned for them on the inside, you could potentially ruin the entire event for them from this point. All it takes is keeping them on a long, slowly-moving and frustrating queue, and they will most likely not enjoy the event. Couple that with them missing some of the planned events just because they were out in the check-in line, and you might have successfully ruined the event with your own hands.

Event check-in software allows you to get out of this bind.

With an intuitive, easy-to-use, smooth and fast interface, every attendee can be registered and checked in in the blink of an eye.

2. Saving on Paper

There is a preference for brands and organizations going green these days, besides the obvious impact that has for our environment too. Thus, you would be killing two birds with one stone when you choose online check-in software.

This is especially true for brands that host multiple events in a single year. The mounds of paper which could have been consumed in registering and checking in each attendee will go a long way in helping us contribute to a better environment. The need to rent event printing device will also be unnecessary.

That leads us to the next point.

3. Better Data Management

Where do we start from?

Paper registrations and check-in do not allow for a certain kind of flexibility. For example, making last minute changes to the attendee list would require the manual entering of their details which could take quite some time. This would not be an issue with online check-in apps which would allow such details to be updated over the air instead.

Furthermore, modifications to existing data is made smoother and neater with check-in software. In practice, they make it easy for the attendee to request and update accommodation status, food preferences, preferred activities and much more. All of that is done in such a way that beats having to cross the details out from a piece of paper or simply issuing another piece of document to that effect.

In the same vein, keeping close track of the data is made possible. While papers can get lost and missing, the information from online options will be stored in the cloud forever. That way, it can always be accessed anywhere and at any time, as long as the viewer has the required permissions to view such data.

4. Improved ROI & Productivity

The event ROI is always a measure of how much was pumped into the event, and what was gained from it in the end. Thinking of how much goes into an event, you also have to consider all the extra hands being hired to perform certain routine tasks.

These positions could easily be eliminated when an online event check-in software is in play. A fewer number of employees will be required to man the check-in stand this time around while the other workforce could be deployed to performing more core, productive tasks.

5. Insights

Data is very important to businesses these days, and you can leverage these event check-in software to better your trade as an event organizer.

With permission from the user, this event check-in software can provide valuable insights into the lives of the attendee. This data can be taken and leveraged for better promotion of future events to ensure improved conversions which leads to a better ROI on the general.

What To Consider When Choosing an Event Check-In App

When selecting an event check-in software, it’s essential to consider several key factors to ensure it meets your needs and enhances the overall experience for both organizers and attendees. A well-chosen app can streamline the registration process, provide valuable real-time data, and offer robust support, ultimately contributing to the success of your event.

1. Ease of Use and Customization

You want an app that’s super easy to use and lets you tweak registration forms to get all the info you need. It should be simple for both you and your attendees, so you don’t run into any problems trying to figure it out.

2. Pre-Event Preparation

Go for an app provider that lets you test everything before the big day to catch any issues early. Ensure the app includes comprehensive training resources for your team so they can manage check-ins efficiently and without confusion.

3. Efficient Check-In Process

Set up efficient check-in stations and consider apps that support self-service kiosks to speed up processing and payment. An app that includes badge printing capabilities for easy attendee identification and enhanced security is even better.

4. Real-Time Data and Monitoring

Select an app that offers real-time data synchronization to maintain accurate records throughout the event. This feature allows you to monitor attendance trends and make necessary adjustments on the fly to improve the event flow.

5. Support and Troubleshooting

Ensure the event check-in software provides 24/7 support to promptly address any technical issues or attendee questions. Having a dedicated support team available ensures a seamless experience for both organizers and attendees.

6. Feedback and Analysis

Pick an event check-in software that lets you collect feedback from your attendees to understand their experience and pinpoint areas for improvement. Analyzing post-event data is key to measuring your success and finding ways to make future events even better.
