Creating A Unique Event App with DUH!

micepad
April 12, 2018
How-To & Tutorials
2 min read

The release of mobile applications for smart devices have taken the market by storm, spanning across various industries and purposes. From the simple to the complex, free-to-use and paid applications, one thing is certain: Mobile apps have increased productivity and profitability for businesses and individuals.

Zooming in to the MICE Industry, mobile applications have not only helped tosave time and money, but also increased participation and excitement among attendees throughinteractive features.Why then are conferences and meetings still organised the old-fashioned way?

Budgetary constraints and infrastructure requirements are an obvious concern. But think about it. How long does it take realistically to develop an application from start to finish? About four to five months! From conceptualisation, to cross platform availability, to user acceptance testing, application development can be extremely complex and time consuming.

A common question we often hear is: What is the lead time for something like this? Our question to you is: How ready is your content?. With the shortest lead time weve had is less than a week from start to finish. Say goodbye to pen and paper and go digital today!

Heres how you do it in a method so simple, we named it Duh!

Design

Have an event graphic, a logo or simply a graphic inspiration? Hand it to us and well design the app to your branding and colours. We make sure that each app we create is unique andconsistent with your branding. Add in other forms of media such as pictures and videos to spruce it up and generate even more buzz, creating an impression that lasts.

Upload

Upload your content in our user-friendly backend or sit back, relax and let us do the work for you. Our experience in handling all forms of content types in countless events will surely help you present your materials in the most effective and visually appealing way possible.

Harvest

You event app is now ready for use. In only a few short weeks, you can harvest the potential of this application and utilise it for your events. Generate valuable insights and create a meaningful event experience for your attendees. Tried and tested by over 20,000 delegates across the world at various events, you are all set to impress. Leave the technical details to us and focus on organising the best event you possibly can!

Have any ideas? Feel free to discuss this with our passionate team here at Micepad. We would be happy to co-operate and build on your ideas!
